Q: Can I donate milk while taking antibiotics?
A: It depends on the antibiotic. Some are safe (e.g., penicillin), others are not (e.g., tetracycline). Always consult the milk bank staff with your prescription.

Q: Is donated breast milk safe for babies?
A: Yes. After screening, testing, and pasteurization, donated milk is very safe and retains most nutritional benefits.
