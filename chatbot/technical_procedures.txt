Blood screening standards for milk donors:
- HIV, Hepatitis B/C: must be negative
- Syphilis test: negative
- Hemoglobin: ≥ 12 g/dL

Microbiological standards for expressed milk:
- Total bacterial count: < 10⁵ CFU/mL
- No pathogenic bacteria (e.g., Salmonella, E. coli)

Pasteurization process (Holder method):
1. Collect milk in sterile containers
2. Cool to 4°C within 4 hours
3. Pasteurize at 62.5°C for 30 minutes
4. Rapid cooling and freeze at -20°C
